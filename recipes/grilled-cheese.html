Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description:

Bread, butter and cheese - here's how to make this classic, yet simply perfect sandwich in a nonstick pan.

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices white bread
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 4 slices Kraft Singles American Cheese

Steps:

  1. Preheat skillet over medium heat
  2. Generously butter one side of each slice of bread (1 tbsp butter per slice)
  3. Place bread with butter side down onto the skillet and add one slice of cheese to each
  4. When cheese starts to melt and bread is starting to lightly golden, flip cheese side of bread onto the other
  5. When bread starts to turn golden-brown, flip over and cook until both sides are golden-brown
